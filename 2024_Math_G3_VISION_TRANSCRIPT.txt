15 bean seeds and will plant all of the seeds. Which set of equations can be used
to find the total number of holes, h, Cecilia will create if she puts exactly 3 seeds
of the same type in each hole?
  A  12 ÷ 3 = 4          C  12 ÷ 3 = 9
     15 ÷ 3 = 5             15 ÷ 3 = 12
     h = 4 + 5              h = 9 × 12

  B  12 ÷ 3 = 4          D  12 ÷ 3 = 9
     15 ÷ 3 = 5             15 ÷ 3 = 12
